# Exercice 4 (6 points)

$$ \mathscr{A} := \lbrace [0, 1], [1, 2[ \rbrace. $$

$$ \tilde{\mathscr{A}} := \lbrace [0,1[, B, ]1, 2[ \rbrace $$

## 4.2
